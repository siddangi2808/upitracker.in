The World's First UPI Expansion Tracker

We track every international bank, payment network, and merchant that accepts UPI outside India — so 32 million Indian travelers always know where India pays.

🎯

Our Purpose

UPI is expanding globally at an unprecedented pace. Yet no single public source tracks where it works, which banks support it, or which merchants accept it.

🌍

Who It's For

Indian travelers abroad, the NRI diaspora, fintech researchers, journalists, travel apps, banks expanding internationally, and policymakers monitoring India's fintech soft power globally.

🔄

How We Stay Current

Data is sourced from NPCI International press releases, RBI circulars, and official MoU announcements — verified by our community before publishing.

🔓

Fully Public

This is a public interest resource. All tracker data is free to browse. A developer API is coming soon for apps wishing to integrate UPI acceptance data.

🤝

Community-First

Updates are submitted by the community — verified travelers, fintech professionals, NRI banking users, and local correspondents across 20+ countries.

📡

No Affiliation

We are not affiliated with NPCI, NIPL, RBI, or any bank. This is an independent tracker built in the public interest.

Powered by the Community

UPI Global Tracker relies on a growing network of contributors — travelers, fintech researchers, bank employees, and journalists.

When a new country goes live or a merchant stops accepting UPI, our community is the first to know. You can be part of that.
